Here are some healthy diet tips:

1. Eat more fruits and vegetables. Try to fill half your plate with fruits and veggies at each meal. They are full of important vitamins, minerals, and fiber.

2. Choose whole grains over refined grains. Opt for whole wheat bread, brown rice, oats, and quinoa instead of white bread, white rice, and other refined grains.

3. Increase protein intake. Lean proteins like chicken, fish, eggs, beans, and lentils can help keep you feeling full.

4. Stay hydrated. Drink plenty of water throughout the day. Avoid sugary drinks like soda.

5. Limit processed and fast foods. These tend to be high in unhealthy fats, added sugars, and calories.

6. Practice portion control. Use a smaller plate and don't overeat, even with healthy foods.

7. Eat slowly and mindfully. This can help you feel more satisfied and prevent overeating.

8. Get enough sleep and manage stress. Lack of sleep and high stress can disrupt your appetite hormones.

9. Plan your meals and snacks ahead of time. This makes it easier to make healthy choices.

10. Find an eating style you can stick to long-term. Fad diets are hard to maintain. Focus on sustainable lifestyle changes.
